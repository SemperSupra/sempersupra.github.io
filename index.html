Warning: experimental / agent-driven repositories

This GitHub organization is used to experiment with software agents and agentic development workflows. Many artifacts may be created or modified by automated systems.

I am not manually reading, reviewing, or validating the code, documentation, releases, containers, or other artifacts in this organization.

Do not trust claims of “production-grade,” “security-hardened,” “verified,” “audited,” or similar language you may see in these repositories. It might be true in some cases, but no effort is being made here to verify it.

Use anything from this organization with caution and at your own risk. You are responsible for independent review, testing, and security assessment before use.

Contributing / issues / pull requests

The participation policy for this organization follows the same model used by WineBot: participation is invite-only. Issues and pull requests from unapproved actors may be automatically labeled and closed.

I may occasionally review automatically closed issues and pull requests, but I make no promises about follow-up, acceptance, or resolution.

Reference policy: WineBot CONTRIBUTING.md

Practical guidance

  • Treat all content as untrusted until you verify it yourself.
  • Prefer pinned commits/tags and reproducible builds.
  • Scan dependencies and container images; run in isolated environments.
